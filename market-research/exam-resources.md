# Websites

## https://www.bjaed.org/

- Free open access anaesthesia review articles containing trusted information on which many exam topics are set. 
- The Articles contain information above which would be expected to be recalled during a viva.
- Articles within the past 12 months are often selected as an exam question within the exam.

##  https://www.openanesthesia.org/ - Login Required

- Provides concise summary documents on over 800 exam relevant topics.
- Summaries structure is conducive to exam questions.
- Provides additional references.

## https://anaesthetists.org/Portals/0/PDFs/QRH/QRH_complete_June_2023.pdf?ver=2023-06-23-141011-603

- Association of Anaesthetists of Great Britain and Ireland Quick Reference Handbook
- A resource on the emergency management of most anaesthetic emergency scenarios

## https://rcoa.ac.uk

- Royal College of Anaesthetists website
- Provides description of the exam structure
- Details the exam curriculum
- Provides practice questions

## FRCA Reveal https://frca-reveal.com/#/

 - Exam revision app used by anaesthetists with practice questions and scenarios.

# Books

## Guide to the FRCA Examination: The Final

- RCoA Published book with example questions in the structure of the exam

## The Clinical Anaesthesia Viva Book: Barker, Mills, Maguire

- Book containing VIVA Questions - Categorised as long and short cases.
- The Short Cases are a mixture of Clinical and Science questions.  They do not categorise them beyond short cases.
- The long cases cover a wide range of common long case scenarios. 

## The Science Viva Book: Bricker

- Contains questions focused on the Clinical science exam questions.
- Categorised as follows: 
    - Anatomy and Its Applications
    - Physiology
    - Pharmacology
    - Physics, Clinical Measurement, Equipment and Statistics

# Masterpass: The Final FRCA Structured Oral Examination

- Book containing VIVA Questions - Categorised under Clinical Viva and Basic Science VIVA.

# Mendonca: The Structured Oral Examination in Clinical Anaesthesia

- Book containing VIVA Questions - Categorised under Long Case, Short Cases and Clinical Science.

# Oxford: Structured Oral Examination Practice for the Final FRCA

- Book containing VIVA Questions - Categorised under Clinical Anaesthesia: Long Case & Short Cases and Clinical Science questions.
